Auto Glass FAQ

Arizona does not mandate free windshield replacement, but many policies with comprehensive coverage cover glass repairs with no deductible. Rock chip repairs are frequently covered at zero out-of-pocket cost. We verify your coverage during the assessment process and handle the claim paperwork.
ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) uses cameras mounted behind your windshield to power automatic braking, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control. After any windshield replacement, these sensors must be recalibrated to manufacturer specs. Any vehicle from approximately 2018 onward with these features requires calibration — failure to calibrate can cause these safety systems to malfunction.
